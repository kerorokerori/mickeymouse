Why you should use OnionWallet:

And with more and more exchanges and other services following AML and KYC policies, its getting really hard to stay anonymous to government agencys when dealing with bitcoins. OnionWallet helps you break that chain, since its hosted at a Tor hidden service, and no one knows who we are, we do not have to follow any AML and KYC policies, so we cannot be forced to give out any of our users information. OnionWallet mixes all bitcoins, which makes tracing you on the Bitcoin blockchain impossible.
